ABOUT US

Take a break Cafe was established in 2001, owned by 3 people . 10 years
later, we’re still happily serving community with quality fresh food. Our cafe
has grown to become one of largest and most recognized services. It’s almost as
popular as our famous desserts!

We serve Filipino food, made with local ingredients, wherever possible, to provide
you with fresher, healthier and deliciously tasty meals. We have a well stocked pastry
cabinet a range of espresso coffees made from the finest coffee beans,
roasted right here, and our baristas really know their
beans. With their superior technical knowledge and a passion for making
amazing coffee they will make you the perfect cuppa.
We host monthly wine dinners, chefs’ tasting dinners and more. We also enjoy hosting your
special occasions – whether baby showers, anniversaries, birthdays, or office parties.

It’s the perfect place to relax on your own or to catch up with friends while enjoying great coffee and great food.

OUR BELIEFS

Our customers have offered us over a decade of memorable moments serving community, and so we believe
the least we can do is continue serving them delicious dishes with a heartfelt smile. Customer service
is a cornerstone of our business, and receiving a smile in return is what we strive for – we hope that
our food will make everyone’s day a little bit better.
As such, we also strive to continuously improve our menu to satisfy your varying needs. From vegetarian,

to gluten free, to diverse ethnic options, we want our customers to feel like We cater to you.

OUR QUALITY

At Take a Break Cafe, we pride ourselves on the quality and freshness of our meals. Everything is made in-house by our chefs each and every day using only the best ingredients. Our owners still bake and cook all of our signature food. And because we also pride ourselves on contributing to our community, we are committed to using as much local produce as possible.
